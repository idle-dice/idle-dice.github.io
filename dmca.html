DMCA Notice and Policy

Our Commitment to Protecting Intellectual Property Rights

At idle-dice.github.io, we place the utmost importance on respecting and protecting the intellectual property rights of content creators, copyright holders, and licensors. We fully comply with the Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA) and other relevant copyright laws worldwide. Our goal is to maintain a responsible platform that honors the legal rights of all parties involved.

Upon receiving a legitimate and complete copyright infringement notification, idle-dice.github.io acts promptly to investigate and, where necessary, remove or disable access to content alleged to infringe upon intellectual property rights. This process ensures that copyright holders can safeguard their works while maintaining a fair and transparent approach.

How to Report Copyright Infringement

If you believe that any content hosted or displayed on idle-dice.github.io infringes your copyright or other intellectual property rights, we encourage you to submit a detailed and formal notification as soon as possible. To enable us to respond effectively and expediently, your notification should include all of the following elements:

  • Your complete legal name, current mailing address, telephone number, and email address where you can be reached for correspondence.

  • A thorough and specific description of the copyrighted work(s) that you claim has been infringed, including any registration number or relevant documentation if applicable.

  • The exact URL(s) on idle-dice.github.io where the allegedly infringing content is located.

  • A statement, made under penalty of perjury, affirming your good faith belief that the disputed use of the material is unauthorized by the copyright owner, its agent, or by law.

  • A declaration that the information provided in the notification is accurate and that you are authorized to act on behalf of the copyright owner.

  • Your physical or electronic signature, which verifies the authenticity of the claim.

Please send your completed copyright infringement notification to our designated DMCA agent using the contact details listed below.

Counter-Notification Procedure

If you are the content provider whose material has been removed or disabled due to a DMCA notice and you believe this action was made in error or due to misidentification, you have the right to submit a counter-notification. This legal process allows you to request the reinstatement of your content.

A valid counter-notice must contain:

  • Your full name, mailing address, telephone number, and email address.

  • Identification of the specific content that was removed or disabled and the URL where it was previously accessible.

  • A statement, made under penalty of perjury, asserting that you have a good faith belief the content was mistakenly removed or disabled.

  • Consent to the jurisdiction of the federal district court for your area (or an appropriate judicial body) and an agreement to accept service of process from the original complaining party.

  • Your handwritten or electronic signature confirming the legitimacy of the counter-notification.

Upon receipt of a proper counter-notification, idle-dice.github.io will promptly forward it to the original complainant. Unless the complainant files a lawsuit within the statutory period to prevent restoration, we will reinstate the content in compliance with the DMCA guidelines.

Policy Regarding Repeat Infringers

We are firmly committed to upholding copyright laws and protecting creators’ rights. Accounts or users who repeatedly violate copyright laws and infringe intellectual property rights on idle-dice.github.io will face strict consequences. These may include suspension, termination of access, or permanent banning from the platform, following the guidelines set forth in the DMCA and our internal enforcement policies.

This policy is critical to maintaining a lawful, ethical, and respectful digital environment for all users and creators.

Contact Information
